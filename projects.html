Charlotte the Spiderbot

Video
Write-Up

The Spiderbot is a toy prototype created for the Fall 2017 course Info 4320 - Rapid Prototyping and Physical Computing. The Spiderbot is a mobile motion-controlled robot. Its primary function is to serve as a toy. Our goal was to create a toy with a contactless interface which translates bodily motion in space to visual output. There already exist many variations on remote-control toys and even contactless digital video games (for example, the Xbox Kinect) but there seems to be a lack of contactless physical toys with a solely creative (as opposed to goal-based) intent--this is what the Spiderbot aspires to be.
